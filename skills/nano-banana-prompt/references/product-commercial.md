# Product & Commercial Photography

## E-commerce Product Isolation

### Professional Product Shot from Casual Photo

**Use case:** Transform messy background photos into clean e-commerce images

**Template:**
```
Identify the main product in the uploaded photo (automatically removing any hands holding it or messy background details).
Recreate it as a premium e-commerce product shot.

Subject Isolation:
- Cleanly extract the product
- Completely remove any fingers, hands, or clutter
- Remove any supporting objects (tables, stands visible in original)
- Product should appear floating/isolated

Background:
- Pure white studio background (RGB 255, 255, 255)
- OR subtle gradient gray background
- Subtle natural contact shadow at base to ground the product
- Shadow should be soft, realistic, directly beneath product

Lighting:
- Soft commercial studio lighting
- Highlight product's texture and material
- Even illumination with no harsh glare
- Slight highlights on reflective surfaces
- Proper exposure across entire product

Retouching:
- Fix any lens distortion from original photo
- Improve sharpness and clarity
- Color-correct to make product look brand new
- Remove any scratches, dust, or imperfections
- Enhance material textures (fabric weave, metal sheen, plastic smoothness)

Composition:
- Product centered in frame
- Appropriate spacing around product
- Proper orientation (upright, at slight angle if appropriate)
- Professional product photography framing

Quality: 4K resolution, professional e-commerce quality, clean and polished
```

**Template:**
```
Subject: A professional financial Sankey diagram visualizing the Income Statement of [COMPANY NAME]

Visual Style:
- High-fidelity vector infographic
- Clean minimalist aesthetic
- Flat design
- Background: Clean very light grey or off-white

Color Strategy (CRITICAL):
1. Analyze [COMPANY NAME] logo
2. Extract primary brand color
3. Use as dominant theme for:
   - Main revenue flows
   - Profit blocks
4. Create harmonious palette based on primary color:
   - Saturated shades for vertical nodes (bars)
   - Semi-transparent lighter opacity gradients for flowing paths
   - Cohesive professional look
   - Avoid clashing colors

Composition & Structure:
- Flow: Horizontal from Left (Revenue Sources) to Right (Net Profit)
- Texture: Silky smooth connecting paths
- Elegant Bezier curves like liquid ribbons

Branding:
- Place official [COMPANY NAME] logo clearly at:
  * Top center, OR
  * Near central revenue bar

Financial Structure:
Clearly distinguish:
1. Revenue (left)
2. Gross Profit
3. Operating Expenses (flowing out)
4. Net Profit (right)

Details:
- High resolution 4K
- Sharp typography (sans-serif)

- Proper numerical labels
- Legend if needed
- Clean, executive presentation quality
