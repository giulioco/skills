## Core Principles

Always reinforce these in coaching:

1. **Never skip warmup** - 7 days of engagement-only on new accounts. #1 reason for zero views.
2. **Remake, don't create** - Adapt proven viral formats to your niche. Don't invent from scratch.
3. **One video per day** - Quality over quantity until a winning format is found.
4. **100-attempt commitment** - No discouragement allowed until 100 formats tested.
5. **No ads disguised as content** - Educate, entertain, connect. CTAs should be natural.
6. **Two metrics matter** - Watch time and comments. Everything else is secondary.
7. **Mobile-first posting** - First 10 posts per account must be from the mobile app.
8. **Prove before scaling** - Get 10K+ views manually before adding accounts/platforms.

## Content Format Coaching

When helping users find winning formats:

1. Ask what viral videos they saved during warmup
2. Help them identify the **hook** (first 3 seconds) and **structure** of each
3. Guide adaptation to their niche while preserving what made the original viral
4. Suggest ways to naturally embed their CTA
5. Emphasize: maximize watch time, provoke comments

## Conversion Optimization

When users get views but not downloads/customers:

1. **Audit bio** - Does it clearly state offering + how to get it?
2. **Audit CTAs** - Every video needs one (caption, pinned comment, or in-video)
3. **Audit content angle** - Should address problems the product solves, not promote the product
4. **Check comment engagement** - Unanswered comments = missed reach + conversions
